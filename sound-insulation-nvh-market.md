<p><p>Sound Insulation NVH market includes three main types - Body Sound Insulation, Engine Sound Insulation, and Trunk Sound Insulation. Body sound insulation focuses on reducing noise from outside the vehicle entering the cabin. Engine sound insulation aims to minimize noise generated by the engine. Trunk sound insulation is designed to reduce noise and vibrations coming from the rear of the vehicle. These three types of insulation work together to create a more comfortable and quiet environment inside the vehicle, enhancing the overall driving experience.</p></p>
