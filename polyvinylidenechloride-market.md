<p><p>Polyvinylidenechloride, also known as PVDC, is a polymer used in various industries. PVDC resins are solid forms of the polymer and are mainly used for packaging materials due to their barrier properties against moisture and gases. On the other hand, PVDC latex is a liquid form of the polymer and is primarily used in coatings and adhesives. Both types of PVDC offer excellent chemical resistance and barrier properties, making them popular choices for a wide range of applications in the market.</p></p>
