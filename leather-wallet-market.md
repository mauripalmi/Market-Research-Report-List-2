<p><p>The leather wallet market can be segmented into two main types: bifold wallets and trifold wallets. Bifold wallets have a simple design with two equal halves that fold in the middle. They are compact and typically have several card slots and a few pockets for cash. Trifold wallets, on the other hand, are slightly bulkier and have three equal sections that fold together. They usually have more card slots, pockets, and compartments for organizing various items. Both bifold and trifold wallets cater to different preferences in terms of design and functionality.</p></p>
