<p><ul><li>Halogen Type</li><li>Halogen Free Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flame Retardant PA6 market can be categorized into two main types: Halogen Type and Halogen Free Type. Halogen Type flame retardants contain halogen elements like bromine or chlorine, which can effectively suppress combustion by releasing halogen radicals. On the other hand, Halogen Free Type flame retardants do not contain any halogen elements and typically use phosphorus or nitrogen-based compounds to achieve flame retardancy. Both types have their advantages and limitations, with Halogen Free Type being increasingly preferred due to concerns over environmental and health impacts of halogen-containing flame retardants.</p></p>
