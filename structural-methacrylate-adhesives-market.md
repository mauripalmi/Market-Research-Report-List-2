<p><p>Structural methacrylate adhesives are available in various ratios for mixing, including 1:1, 4:1, and 10:1. Combined at a 1:1 ratio, the adhesive offers a balanced mix for quick curing and high strength. At a 4:1 ratio, the adhesive provides a faster curing time and excellent gap filling properties. In contrast, a 10:1 ratio offers a longer working time and is ideal for applications where precision is required. These different ratios cater to a variety of bonding needs in different industries.</p></p>
