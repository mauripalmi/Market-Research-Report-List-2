<p><p>Eco-friendly tiles come in various types such as ceramic, travertine, porcelain, terrazzo, and glass. Ceramic tiles are made from natural clay and minerals, while travertine tiles are quarried from natural stone. Porcelain tiles are highly durable and sustainable, made from a mix of clay and other natural materials. Terrazzo tiles are made from a combination of recycled materials such as glass, marble, and quartz. Glass tiles are eco-friendly, as they can be made from recycled glass and are easy to maintain and clean.</p></p>
