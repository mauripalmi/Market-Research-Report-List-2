<p><ul><li>Permanent IVC Filters</li><li>Retrievable IVC Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nitinol based filters are commonly used in the medical field for treating patients with venous diseases. There are two main types of Nitinol based filters in the market: Permanent IVC Filters and Retrievable IVC Filters. Permanent IVC Filters are designed to stay in the body permanently to prevent blood clots from reaching the lungs. On the other hand, Retrievable IVC Filters are temporary and can be removed once the risk of blood clots has subsided. Both types of filters are effective in reducing the risk of pulmonary embolism in patients.</p></p>
