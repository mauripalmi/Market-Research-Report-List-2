<p><p>Condensed whey is a byproduct of cheese production and comes in three main market types - plain, sweetened, and acid. Plain condensed whey has no added sugars or flavorings, making it versatile for cooking and baking. Sweetened condensed whey has added sugar, making it ideal for desserts and beverages. Acid condensed whey is created by adding acid during processing, giving it a tangy flavor that works well in savory dishes. Each market type offers different flavor profiles and uses for consumers.</p></p>
