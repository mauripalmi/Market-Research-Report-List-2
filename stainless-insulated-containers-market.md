<p><strong>The Stainless Insulated Containers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Vacuum Stainless Insulated Containers</li><li>Vacuum Stainless Insulated Containers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-vacuum stainless insulated containers are designed with a double-wall construction, providing basic insulation to keep hot or cold temperatures for a limited time. These containers are suitable for storing food or beverages for short durations. Vacuum stainless insulated containers, on the other hand, have a vacuum-sealed insulation layer between the double walls, offering superior temperature retention for longer periods. These containers are ideal for maintaining the desired temperature of liquids or food for extended periods, making them suitable for outdoor activities or travel.</p></p>
