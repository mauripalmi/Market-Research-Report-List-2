<p><p>C Grade hollow sucker rods are made of carbon steel with lower tensile strength and are suitable for shallow and low-pressure wells. D Grade hollow sucker rods are made of high-quality alloy steel with medium tensile strength and are suitable for moderate depth and pressure wells. H Grade hollow sucker rods are made of premium alloy steel with high tensile strength and are suitable for deep and high-pressure wells. Each grade offers different levels of durability and performance based on the specific well conditions they are designed for.</p></p>
