<p><p>Serrated face nuts are available in two main types - single-sided serrated nut and double-sided serrated nut. Single-sided serrated nuts have serrations on only one side, providing a secure grip against the mating surface. Double-sided serrated nuts have serrations on both sides, offering even more stability and security. Both types are commonly used in industries where vibration resistance and locking performance are important, such as automotive, machinery, and construction.</p></p>
