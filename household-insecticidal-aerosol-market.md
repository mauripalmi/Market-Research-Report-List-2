<p><p>Household insecticidal aerosol market includes foam aerosols and powder aerosols. Foam aerosols are designed to expand upon contact with surfaces, allowing for better coverage and longer-lasting effects. They are commonly used in hard-to-reach areas such as cracks and crevices. Powder aerosols, on the other hand, release a fine powder upon spraying, which adheres to surfaces and effectively eliminates insects upon contact. Both types of aerosols are convenient and easy to use for controlling pest infestations in households.</p></p>
