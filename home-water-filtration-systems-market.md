<p><p>Home water filtration systems are available in various types to cater to different needs of consumers. Reverse osmosis (RO) filtration systems remove impurities through a semipermeable membrane. UV filtration systems use ultraviolet light to kill bacteria and viruses in the water. Carbon filtration systems use activated carbon to absorb impurities and improve taste. Other types of filtration systems may include distillation, ion exchange, and ceramic filters. Each type of filtration system offers unique benefits and caters to specific contaminants in the water.</p></p>
