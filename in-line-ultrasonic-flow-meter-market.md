<p><strong>The In Line Ultrasonic Flow Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single/Dual Path</li><li>Multi Path</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-line ultrasonic flow meters are categorized into single/dual path and multi-path types. Single/dual path meters have one or two transducers that emit and receive ultrasonic signals, providing accurate flow measurements in straight pipe sections. Multi-path meters have multiple transducers that emit signals at different angles, allowing for more accurate measurements in complex flow conditions. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
