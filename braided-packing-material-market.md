<p><p>Braided packing materials are used to seal gaps and prevent leakage in various industrial applications. Carbon packing is known for its high temperature resistance, PTFE packing offers excellent chemical resistance, and graphite packing provides superior lubrication properties. Asbestos packing, although less common due to health concerns, offers good heat resistance. PTFE blends packing combine the benefits of PTFE with other materials for enhanced performance. Other types of braided packing materials include aramid fibers, fiberglass, and ceramic fibers, each offering unique properties for specific applications.</p></p>
