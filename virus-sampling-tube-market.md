<p><p>Virus Sampling Tubes can be classified into two main types based on their processing methods: Inactivated and Non-inactivated. Inactivated tubes contain chemicals that kill the virus, making it safe for handling and transport. These tubes are suitable for study purposes where live virus is not required. Non-inactivated tubes do not contain any chemicals to kill the virus, preserving it in its original state. These tubes are typically used in research and diagnostic applications where live virus is needed for further analysis.</p></p>
