<p><p>A PLC (Planar Lightwave Circuit) Optical Splitter is a passive optical component used to split or combine optical signals. It functions by utilizing the waveguide technology to divide the input signal into multiple output signals or combine multiple input signals into one output signal. PLC Optical Splitters are widely used in various applications such as fiber to the home (FTTH), passive optical networks (PON), and telecommunications.</p><p>The PLC Optical Splitter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for high-speed internet services, the adoption of fiber optic networks for higher bandwidth requirements, and the growing deployment of cloud-based services are driving the growth of the market. Additionally, the rising demand for efficient and cost-effective optical splitters in data centers, telecommunications networks, and CATV networks is fueling market growth.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the PLC Optical Splitter Market include the development of smaller form factor splitters, the introduction of PLC splitters with higher split ratios, and the integration of PLC splitters with other optical components for improved performance.
