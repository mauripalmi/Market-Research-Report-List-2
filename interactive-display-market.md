<p><p>Interactive display market can be classified into two main types - LCD and LED displays. LCD displays use liquid crystal technology to produce images, offering high resolution and viewing angles. On the other hand, LED displays use light emitting diodes to create images, providing brighter and more energy-efficient screens. Both technologies are commonly used in interactive displays for applications such as digital signage, kiosks, and interactive whiteboards. The choice between LCD and LED displays often depends on factors such as budget, space requirements, and desired image quality.</p></p>
