<p><strong>The Steel Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Left Regular Lay</li><li>Left Lang Lay</li><li>Right Regular Lay</li><li>Right Lang Lay</li><li>Alternate Lay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steel cable market types refer to different configurations of wires in the cable construction. Left Regular Lay features strands laid in the same direction as the cable, while Left Lang Lay has strands laid opposite to the cable direction. Right Regular Lay and Right Lang Lay are similar to their left counterparts but with strands laid in the opposite direction. Alternate Lay combines left and right lay strands for enhanced strength and flexibility. Each type caters to specific industry needs and applications.</p></p>
