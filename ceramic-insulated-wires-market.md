<p><p>Ceramic insulated wires market is segmented into three types: Round Wire, Stranded Wire, and Others. Round Wire is a single, solid wire with ceramic insulation, commonly used in low voltage applications. Stranded Wire consists of multiple smaller wires twisted together and covered with ceramic insulation, providing more flexibility and durability. Other types of ceramic insulated wires may include twisted pair or coaxial cables, offering different characteristics depending on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
