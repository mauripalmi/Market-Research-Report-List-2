<p><p>Carbon Fiber-reinforced Plastic (CFRP) is a composite material consisting of carbon fibers embedded in a polymer matrix. There are various types of CFRP based on the type of precursor material used for the carbon fibers. PAN-based CFRP is the most common and widely used type due to its excellent mechanical properties. Pitch-based CFRP offers high thermal conductivity, while Rayon-based CFRP is known for its lower cost. Other types of CFRP may include blends of different precursors or specialized formulations for specific applications.</p></p>
