<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Alloy Wheel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Casting</li><li>Forging</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial vehicle alloy wheel market can be segmented into three main types: casting, forging, and other. Casting involves pouring molten metal into a mold to create the desired wheel shape, while forging involves using intense pressure to shape the metal into the desired form. The "other" category includes alternative methods of manufacturing alloy wheels such as flow forming and rotary forging. Each type of production method has its own benefits and drawbacks in terms of cost, strength, and design flexibility.</p></p>
