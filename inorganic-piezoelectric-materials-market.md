<p><p>Inorganic Piezoelectric Materials Market consists of two main types: Piezoelectric Crystals and Piezoelectric Ceramics. Piezoelectric crystals are natural or synthetic materials that produce an electric charge in response to mechanical stress. They are commonly used in sensors and transducers. Piezoelectric ceramics, on the other hand, are man-made materials with similar properties to crystals but are manufactured through a controlled process. They are widely used in medical ultrasound devices, pressure sensors, and actuators due to their high piezoelectric coefficients and mechanical strength.</p></p>
