<p><strong>The SBR Rubber Compound Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>E-SBR</li><li>S-SBR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SBR Rubber Compound market is classified into two main types: E-SBR and S-SBR. E-SBR (emulsion styrene-butadiene rubber) is produced through emulsion polymerization and is known for its good abrasion resistance and elasticity, making it suitable for tire treads and footwear applications. On the other hand, S-SBR (solution styrene-butadiene rubber) is manufactured via solution polymerization and offers superior performance in terms of rolling resistance and wet grip, making it a preferred choice for high-performance tires, such as those used in sports cars.</p></p>
