<p><p>Handbike market is divided into three main types based on propulsion systems: manual, electric, and hybrid. The manual handbikes are propelled solely by the user's physical strength, offering a traditional workout experience. Electric handbikes are powered by a motor, providing assistance to the user for longer distances or uphill climbs. Hybrid handbikes combine both manual and electric systems, allowing users to switch between the two modes based on their preference or needs. Each type offers unique benefits and caters to different user preferences and abilities.</p></p>
