<p><p>The automotive refinish market can be categorized into three main types: Solvent Borne, Water Borne, and Others. Solvent Borne refers to traditional coatings that use solvent-based chemicals for application. Water Borne, on the other hand, uses water as a carrier for the coating, making it more environmentally friendly. The Others category includes newer technologies such as UV-cured coatings, powder coatings, and high-solid coatings. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages, catering to different preferences and needs in the automotive refinish industry.</p></p>
