<p><ul><li>Radial Fans Seats</li><li>Axial Fans Seats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ventilated seats market includes two main types: Radial fans seats and Axial fans seats. Radial fans seats have fans that move air outward from the center of the seat, providing a more even distribution of airflow. On the other hand, Axial fans seats have fans that move air parallel to the axis of the seat, creating a column of airflow. Both types of ventilated seats offer comfort and cooling benefits to users, making them popular choices in the automotive industry.</p></p>
