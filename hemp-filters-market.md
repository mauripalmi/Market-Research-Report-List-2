<p><p>HEMP filters are used in both single-phase and three-phase power systems to protect sensitive electronic equipment from electromagnetic interference. Single-phase filters are designed for applications where only one line and one neutral conductor are present, such as in residential setups. Three-phase filters are used in industrial settings where three power lines and a neutral conductor are present. Both types of filters offer effective filtering of electromagnetic interference to ensure the smooth operation of equipment and prevent damage from power disturbances.</p></p>
