<p><p>Sprue pickers are automated devices used in plastic injection molding to remove excess plastic from molds. There are two main market types for sprue pickers: pneumatic and electric. Pneumatic sprue pickers are powered by compressed air and are known for their reliability and cost-effectiveness. Electric sprue pickers, on the other hand, are powered by electricity and offer more precise control and flexibility. Both types of sprue pickers are widely used in the manufacturing industry to improve efficiency and accuracy in the molding process.</p></p>
