<p><p>Elastomer foam materials are categorized into different types based on the type of polymer used in their production. NBR (Nitrile Butadiene Rubber) based elastomeric foam is known for its excellent oil resistance. EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) based foam offers outstanding weathering and UV resistance. Chloroprene based foam is commonly used for its flame retardant properties. Other types of elastomeric foams include blends and specialty formulations tailored to specific applications, offering a wide range of properties to meet various industrial needs.</p></p>
