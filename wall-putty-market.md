<p><p>Wall putty market consists of two main types: Interior Wall Putty Powder and Exterior Wall Putty Powder. Interior wall putty is specially formulated for indoor applications and provides a smooth finish to walls. Exterior wall putty is designed to withstand weather elements and protect walls from moisture and harsh conditions. Both types of wall putty powders are essential for preparing walls before painting, ensuring a durable and attractive finish. The choice between the two types depends on the specific requirements of the surface to be coated.</p></p>
