<p><p>Transmitting antennas in the HF (High Frequency), VHF (Very High Frequency), and UHF (Ultra High Frequency) markets are designed to transmit radio waves at specific frequency ranges. HF antennas operate in the 3-30 MHz range, suitable for long-distance communication. VHF antennas operate in the 30-300 MHz range, commonly used for FM radio and television broadcasts. UHF antennas operate in the 300 MHz-3 GHz range, ideal for shorter-range communication such as mobile phones and digital television. Each market type serves different communication needs based on the frequency range they operate within.</p></p>
