<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Hearing Implants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cochlear implants</li><li>Mid-Ear Implants</li><li>Bone Anchored Hearing Aids (BAHA)</li><li>Auditory Brainstem Implant (ABI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hearing implants are devices used to help individuals with hearing loss. Cochlear implants are surgically implanted devices that stimulate the auditory nerve. Mid-Ear implants are placed in the middle ear to improve sound transmission. Bone Anchored Hearing Aids (BAHA) are attached to the skull to bypass the outer or middle ear. Auditory Brainstem Implants (ABI) are placed on the brainstem for individuals who cannot benefit from cochlear implants. Each type of hearing implant serves a specific purpose in addressing different types of hearing loss.</p></p>
