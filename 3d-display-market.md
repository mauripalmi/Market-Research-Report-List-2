<p><p>There are four main types of 3D display technologies in the market: Digital Light Processing (DLP), Plasma Display Panel (PDP), Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED), and Light Emitting Diode (LED). DLP uses micro mirrors to generate images, PDP uses charged gas to produce light, OLED utilizes organic compounds that emit light when stimulated, and LED uses a backlighting system with light emitting diodes. Each technology offers unique features and benefits, catering to different preferences and requirements in the 3D display market.</p></p>
