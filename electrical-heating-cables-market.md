<p><p>Electrical heating cables are categorized into four types: self-regulating electric heating cable, constant wattage electric heating cable, mineral insulated electric heating cable, and skin-effect electric heating cable. Self-regulating cables adjust their heat output based on temperatures, making them energy efficient. Constant wattage cables provide a consistent level of heat output. Mineral insulated cables are constructed with a metallic sheath for high-temperature applications. Skin-effect cables are designed for heating pipelines and tanks. Each type offers unique benefits for different heating requirements.</p></p>
