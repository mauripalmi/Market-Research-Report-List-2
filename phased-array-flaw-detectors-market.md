<p><p>Phased array flaw detectors are used in non-destructive testing to detect and evaluate flaws in materials. There are two main types of phased array flaw detectors - linear array and circular array. </p><p>Linear array flaw detectors use a series of transducer elements arranged in a straight line to emit and receive ultrasonic waves. Circular array flaw detectors use a circular arrangement of transducer elements to achieve a 360-degree inspection coverage. Both types offer high resolution imaging and increased inspection efficiency, making them popular choices in industries such as aerospace, manufacturing, and automotive.</p></p>
