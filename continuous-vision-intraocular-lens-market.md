<p><p>Continuous Vision Intraocular Lens are a type of multifocal intraocular lens that provide seamless vision at all distances. They are designed to correct presbyopia and other refractive errors, allowing patients to see clearly without the need for glasses or contact lenses. </p><p>There are two main types of Continuous Vision Intraocular Lenses: hydrophobic acrylic IOLs and hydrophilic acrylic IOLs. Hydrophobic acrylic IOLs are known for their durability and resistance to protein or lipid deposits, while hydrophilic acrylic IOLs have a higher water content and are softer, providing excellent biocompatibility and flexibility. Both types of IOLs have their distinct advantages and are chosen based on the patient's individual needs and preferences.</p></p>
