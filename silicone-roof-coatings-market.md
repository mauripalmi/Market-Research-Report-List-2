<p><ul><li>Anti-Corrosion Coatings</li><li>Silicone Waterproofing Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone roof coatings are divided into two main types: Anti-Corrosion Coatings and Silicone Waterproofing Coatings. Anti-Corrosion Coatings protect metal roofs from corrosion, extending their lifespan. These coatings create a barrier that prevents moisture and other corrosive elements from reaching the metal surface. On the other hand, Silicone Waterproofing Coatings provide a protective layer that prevents water infiltration, making them ideal for flat or low-sloped roofs. These coatings also offer UV resistance, helping to extend the life of the roof.</p></p>
