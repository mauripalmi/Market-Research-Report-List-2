<p><p>Yeast market is primarily categorized into two types: Fresh Yeast and Dry Yeast. Fresh yeast, also known as compressed yeast, is moist and needs to be refrigerated. It is often used in bakeries for making bread due to its strong leavening power. Dry yeast, on the other hand, is dehydrated and has a longer shelf life. It is commonly used in home baking and is available in various forms such as active dry yeast and instant yeast. Both types of yeast play a crucial role in fermentation processes in food production.</p></p>
