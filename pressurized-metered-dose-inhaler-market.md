<p><p>Pressurized Metered-dose Inhalers are classified into two main types based on their design and functionality: Single-dose Inhalers and Multi-dose Inhalers. Single-dose inhalers contain a pre-measured dose of medication in each canister, which is released with each actuation. Multi-dose inhalers, on the other hand, have the capability to deliver multiple doses of medication from a single canister. Both types of inhalers are widely used in the treatment of respiratory conditions such as asthma and COPD.</p></p>
