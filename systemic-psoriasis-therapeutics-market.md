<p><p>Systemic Psoriasis Therapeutics market includes two main types of treatments: Biologics and Small Molecules. Biologics are derived from living organisms and target specific parts of the immune system involved in psoriasis, while Small Molecules are synthetic compounds that also work on the immune system but through a different mechanism. Biologics are typically more expensive and administered through injections or infusions, while Small Molecules are taken orally. Both types of treatments have shown effectiveness in managing symptoms of psoriasis.</p></p>
