<p><p>Hydraulic Hand Pump market offers two main types: Single Acting and Double Acting. Single Acting Hydraulic Hand Pump operates in one direction, providing hydraulic fluid output when the handle is pressed down. On the other hand, Double Acting Hydraulic Hand Pump operates in both directions, delivering hydraulic fluid output when the handle is pushed or pulled. Both types are commonly used in various applications that require manual hydraulic power, such as construction equipment, rescue operations, and industrial machinery.</p></p>
