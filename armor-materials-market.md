<p><ul><li>Aramid</li><li>Composites</li><li>Metals & Alloys</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Armor Materials Market can be categorized into four main types: Aramid, Composites, Metals & Alloys, and Others. Aramid materials, such as Kevlar, are known for their high strength and resistance to abrasion. Composites are materials made from two or more different materials, combining their strengths. Metals & Alloys include traditional armor materials like steel and titanium. The category of Others encompasses a variety of materials not falling into the other three categories, such as ceramics and glass. Each type offers unique characteristics suited for different applications in the armor industry.</p></p>
