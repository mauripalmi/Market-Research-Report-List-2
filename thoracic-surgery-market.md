<p><p>Thoracic surgery market includes various types such as Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting (CABG), Heart Valve Repair, Transcatheter Heart Valve Replacement (TAVR), Left Atrial Appendage (LAA) Closure, and Thoracic Endovascular Aortic Repair (TEVAR). CABG involves rerouting blood flow around blocked arteries, while Heart Valve Repair focuses on fixing damaged heart valves. TAVR is a minimally invasive procedure to replace a diseased heart valve. LAA Closure prevents blood clots in the heart, and TEVAR treats thoracic aortic aneurysms.</p></p>
