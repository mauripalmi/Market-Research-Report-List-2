<p><p>Aluminum Clad Wire Market consists of two main types - Type AC and Type MC. Type AC refers to aluminum clad copper wire, which is a combination of aluminum and copper, providing the conductivity of copper with the light weight and cost-effectiveness of aluminum. Type MC, on the other hand, stands for metal-clad cable, which features aluminum armor for enhanced durability and protection. Both types of aluminum clad wire are commonly used in various electrical applications, offering different benefits depending on the specific requirements of the project.</p></p>
