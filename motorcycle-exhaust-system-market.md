<p><p>Single exhaust systems refer to motorcycles that have only one exhaust pipe attached to the engine, typically found on smaller displacement bikes. On the other hand, dual exhaust systems are equipped with two exhaust pipes, one on each side of the vehicle, commonly seen on high-performance motorcycles. Single exhaust systems are more common and cost-effective, while dual exhaust systems are favored for their improved engine efficiency and performance. Both types cater to different preferences and needs of motorcycle riders in the market.</p></p>
