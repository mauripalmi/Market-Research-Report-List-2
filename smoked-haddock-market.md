<p><p>Smoked Haddock is available in two main market types: hot-smoked and cold-smoked. Hot-smoked haddock is cooked during the smoking process, resulting in a firmer texture and stronger smoky flavor. This variety is typically ready to eat straight out of the package. On the other hand, cold-smoked haddock is smoked at a lower temperature, giving it a delicate texture and milder smoky taste. It is usually served raw or lightly cooked before consumption. Both types offer unique flavor profiles and can be used in a variety of dishes.</p></p>
