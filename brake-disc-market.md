<p><p>The brake disc market includes two main types: cast iron discs and carbon ceramic matrix composite (CMC) discs. Cast iron discs are widely used due to their cost-effectiveness and durability, making them ideal for everyday driving conditions. On the other hand, CMC discs are lighter, have better thermal conductivity, and offer superior performance under extreme conditions. While CMC discs are more expensive, they are increasingly being adopted in high-performance vehicles and racing cars for their advanced technological features.</p></p>
