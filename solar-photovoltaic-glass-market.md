Tempered glass is heat-treated for increased strength and safety. TCO glass has thin transparent conductive oxide coating for conductivity. Other types include patterned, colored, and anti-soiling glass. Each type offers unique benefits for solar panel applications, with features like improved efficiency, durability, and aesthetics. The diverse range of solar photovoltaic glass caters to a wide range of needs in the market.</p></p>
