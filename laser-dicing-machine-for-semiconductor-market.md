<p><p>Laser dicing machine for semiconductor market includes two main types: Laser Ablation Dicing and Stealth Dicing. Laser Ablation Dicing uses high-powered lasers to ablate the material, resulting in precise and clean cuts without any physical contact. On the other hand, Stealth Dicing utilizes a laser-induced thermal shock process to separate the material along predefined scribe lines. Both methods offer advantages such as increased accuracy, reduced material waste, and improved throughput, making them popular choices in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
