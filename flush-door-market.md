<p><ul><li>Single Doors</li><li>Multi-Doors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flush doors are categorized into two main types based on the number of leaves: single doors and multi-doors. Single doors refer to individual doors that are typically used in residential settings or as entrances to rooms. On the other hand, multi-doors consist of multiple leaves that are hinged together and are commonly used in commercial or industrial applications where larger openings need to be covered. Both types of flush doors provide a sleek and minimalistic appearance while offering durability and functionality.</p></p>
