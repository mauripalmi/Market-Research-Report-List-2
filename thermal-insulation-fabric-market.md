<p><p>Thermal insulation fabric market is segmented into two main types: glass fiber and mineral wool. Glass fiber insulation is made from molten glass spun into fibers, offering high thermal resistance and durability. Mineral wool insulation, on the other hand, is made from natural rock or slag processed into fibers, providing excellent fire resistance and soundproofing properties. Both types are widely used in construction and industrial applications to reduce heat transfer and improve energy efficiency in buildings.</p></p>
