<p><ul><li>Shunt Switch</li><li>Series Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MEMS switches can be classified into two main market types: shunt switches and series switches. Shunt switches are used to redirect signals in circuits by shunting the signal path when the switch is turned on. On the other hand, series switches are used to interrupt or connect the signal path in series. Both types of MEMS switches play a crucial role in telecommunications, aerospace, and automotive industries where high-performance and reliable switching capabilities are required.</p></p>
