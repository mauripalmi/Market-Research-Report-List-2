<p><p>Micro syringe pumps are devices used in various industries for precise and controlled delivery of liquids. They come in different configurations, with the most common types being 1-channel and 2-channel pumps. </p><p>1-channel pumps have a single channel for dispensing liquids, making them suitable for applications where only one liquid needs to be delivered. </p><p>On the other hand, 2-channel pumps have two separate channels, allowing for the concurrent delivery of two different liquids. This makes them ideal for applications requiring the simultaneous administration of multiple liquids.</p></p>
