<p><p>The Military Aircraft Turbine Engine Market consists of two main types: Turbojet engines and Turbofan engines. Turbojet engines operate by taking in air, compressing it, mixing it with fuel, igniting the mixture, and then expelling the hot exhaust gases out of the back to generate thrust. Turbofan engines work similarly but have an additional fan at the front that helps to produce more thrust through bypass air that flows around the engine core. Both types of engines are commonly used in military aircraft for propulsion.</p></p>
