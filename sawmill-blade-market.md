<p><p>Sawmill blade market consists of two main types: circular saw blades and band saw blades. Circular saw blades are disc-shaped and are commonly used for cutting timber in sawmills. They are versatile and can cut through a variety of materials quickly. Band saw blades, on the other hand, are long, continuous loop blades that are ideal for cutting irregular shapes and intricate designs. Both types of blades play a crucial role in the timber industry by efficiently cutting and shaping wood products.</p></p>
