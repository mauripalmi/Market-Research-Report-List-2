<p><p>Paint additives are crucial components in the formulation of paint products. Among the various market types, rheology modification additives play a key role in controlling the flow and leveling properties of the paint. Biocides are used to prevent microbial growth and extend the shelf life of the paint. Anti-foaming additives are utilized to reduce foam formation during manufacturing and application. Wetting and dispersion additives improve the distribution of pigments in the paint. Impact modification additives enhance the durability and impact resistance of the paint film.</p></p>
