<p><p>Synthetic Dimethicone is available in three main types based on viscosity: Low Viscosity Dimethicone, Medium Viscosity Dimethicone, and High Viscosity Dimethicone. Low Viscosity Dimethicone is lighter and less greasy, making it suitable for skincare products and cosmetics. Medium Viscosity Dimethicone offers a balance between texture and spreadability, making it ideal for hair care products. High Viscosity Dimethicone is thick and provides intense hydration, making it suitable for products like lotions and creams that require long-lasting moisture. Each type caters to different formulation needs in the market.</p></p>
