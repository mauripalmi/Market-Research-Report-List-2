<p><strong>The Projector for Volumetric Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Light Processing (DLP) Technology</li><li>Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCOS) Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital Light Processing (DLP) Technology in the projector for volumetric display market utilizes micro-mirrors to reflect light and create images. It offers high resolution and brightness, making it ideal for large-scale displays. On the other hand, Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCOS) Technology uses liquid crystal panels on top of a silicon backplane to produce images. LCOS projectors offer high image quality, color accuracy, and contrast ratio, making them suitable for applications requiring precise and detailed visuals.</p></p>
