<p><strong>The Internal Combustion Engine Camshafts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Overhead Camshaft (SOHC)</li><li>Double Overhead Camshaft (DOHC)</li><li>Overhead Valve (OHV) Camshafts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the internal combustion engine camshaft market, three main types exist: Single Overhead Camshaft (SOHC), Double Overhead Camshaft (DOHC), and Overhead Valve (OHV) camshafts. SOHC engines have one camshaft per engine bank, while DOHC engines have two camshafts per bank, offering better performance. OHV engines have camshafts located in the engine block, actuating valves through pushrods and rocker arms. Each type of camshaft has its own advantages and is used in different engine configurations based on performance and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
