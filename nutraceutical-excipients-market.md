<p><p>Nutraceutical excipients are substances used in the production of nutraceutical products to improve their stability, solubility, and bioavailability. The market for nutraceutical excipients can be classified into two types: dry and liquid. </p><p>Dry nutraceutical excipients include fillers, binders, and disintegrants, which are typically powders or granules added to solid dosage forms. Liquid nutraceutical excipients, on the other hand, include solvents, surfactants, and emulsifiers used in the production of liquid nutraceutical formulations. Both types of excipients play a crucial role in enhancing the efficacy and shelf-life of nutraceutical products.</p></p>
