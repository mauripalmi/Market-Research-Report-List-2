<p><p>Polyurethane sealants for marine applications come in two market types: single component and multi-component. Single component sealants are ready to use out of the tube and cure upon exposure to moisture in the air. They are easy to use but may have shorter shelf life. Multi-component sealants require mixing different components before application and offer superior performance and durability. They are ideal for more demanding marine applications where strength and longevity are crucial.</p></p>
