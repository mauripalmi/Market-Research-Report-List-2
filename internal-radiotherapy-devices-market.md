<p><p>Internal radiotherapy devices market includes two main types - HDR (High Dose Rate) Afterloaders and PDR (Pulsed Dose Rate) Afterloaders. </p><p>HDR Afterloaders deliver a high dose of radiation in a short amount of time, allowing for precise targeting of tumors while minimizing damage to surrounding healthy tissue. PDR Afterloaders, on the other hand, deliver radiation in pulses, providing a continuous but lower dose rate over a longer period of time. Both types of afterloaders are used in the treatment of various cancers, offering different advantages depending on the specific clinical needs of the patient.</p></p>
