<p><p>Handheld Vital Signs Monitors come in two main types, Traditional Monitors and Microwave Monitors. Traditional Monitors utilize standard medical technologies, such as sensors and displays, to measure vital signs like heart rate, blood pressure, and oxygen levels. In contrast, Microwave Monitors use advanced microwave technology to non-invasively measure vital signs through clothing and even walls. Both types of monitors are compact, portable, and user-friendly, making them ideal for monitoring patients' health in various settings like hospitals, clinics, ambulances, and homes.</p></p>
