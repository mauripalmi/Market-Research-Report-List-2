<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Offline Controllers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Current Mode</li><li>Voltage Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Offline controllers are used in switch-mode power supplies to regulate voltage output. Current mode controllers sense and respond to changes in current flowing through the circuit, providing faster transient response and better stability. Voltage mode controllers, on the other hand, sense and respond to changes in the output voltage, offering greater precision and accuracy in voltage regulation. Both types of controllers have their own advantages and are used in various applications depending on the specific requirements of the power supply design.</p></p>
