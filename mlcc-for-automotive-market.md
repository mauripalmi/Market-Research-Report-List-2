<p><p>MLCCs (Multilayer Ceramic Capacitors) for the automotive market are differentiated based on their capacitance levels. Low capacitance MLCCs are used for applications requiring smaller capacitance values, such as noise filtering and decoupling in electronic systems. On the other hand, high capacitance MLCCs are ideal for applications needing larger capacitance values, such as power supply decoupling and energy storage. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring stable and efficient operation of automotive electronic systems.</p></p>
