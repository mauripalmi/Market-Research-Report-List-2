<p><p>Sensor flushometers are available in three main types: Exposed, Recessed, and Wall-mounted. Exposed flushometers are installed directly on the outside of the toilet fixture, easily visible and accessible. Recessed flushometers are built into the wall behind the toilet, offering a more seamless and streamlined look. Wall-mounted flushometers are attached to the wall above or behind the toilet, providing flexibility in placement and design. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the specific requirements and aesthetics of the restroom space.</p></p>
