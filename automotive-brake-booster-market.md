<p><p>The automotive brake booster market can be categorized into three main types - single diaphragm booster, dual diaphragm booster, and others. Single diaphragm boosters have a single rubber diaphragm which compresses air to assist in braking. Dual diaphragm boosters have two diaphragms for increased braking power and efficiency. The "others" category may include different variations or combinations of diaphragms, as well as any new innovative technologies that do not fit into the single or dual diaphragm categories.</p></p>
