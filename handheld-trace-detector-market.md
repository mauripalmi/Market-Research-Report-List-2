<p><p>The handheld trace detector market consists of various types, including Mass Spectroscopy, Ion Mobility Spectrometry, and Others. Mass Spectroscopy devices analyze the mass of molecules to detect trace substances. Ion Mobility Spectrometry devices separate and detect ions based on their mobility in a carrier gas. Other types of handheld trace detectors may include technologies like infrared spectroscopy or Raman spectroscopy. Each type offers different capabilities and sensitivity levels in detecting trace substances for various applications such as security screening or forensic analysis.</p></p>
