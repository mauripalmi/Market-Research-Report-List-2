<p><strong>The Achromatic Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Achromatic Lenses</li><li>Negative Achromatic Lenses</li><li>Cylindrical Achromatic Lenses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Achromatic lenses are commonly categorized into three main types: Positive Achromatic Lenses, Negative Achromatic Lenses, and Cylindrical Achromatic Lenses. Positive achromatic lenses are designed to converge light rays, providing magnification and focusing capabilities. Negative achromatic lenses diverge light rays, resulting in a reduced image size. Cylindrical achromatic lenses are specifically used to correct astigmatism in optical systems. Each type of achromatic lens serves a different purpose in various applications such as microscopy, photography, and laser systems.</p></p>
