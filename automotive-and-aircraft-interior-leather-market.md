<p><p>Automotive and Aircraft Interior Leather market consists of three main types: Pigmented Leather, Aniline Leather, and Semi Aniline Leather. Pigmented Leather is coated with a layer of pigment which gives it a durable and uniform appearance. Aniline Leather is the most natural-looking type, as it is dyed with transparent dyes that preserve the hide's natural markings. Semi Aniline Leather is a blend of the previous two types, offering a balance between durability and natural aesthetics. Each type caters to different preferences in terms of appearance, durability, and maintenance.</p></p>
