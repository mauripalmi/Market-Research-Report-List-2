<p><p>Medical cyber security market can be categorized into two main types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises solutions involve installing and maintaining security measures within the healthcare facility's own physical infrastructure. In contrast, cloud-based solutions involve outsourcing security measures to a third-party provider who hosts and manages security services remotely. Both types offer different advantages and challenges, with on-premises solutions providing more control and customization, while cloud-based solutions offer scalability and flexibility. Healthcare organizations must carefully consider their specific needs and resources when choosing between these two market types.</p></p>
