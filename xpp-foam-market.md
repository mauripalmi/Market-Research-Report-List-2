<p><p>XPP foam, or cross-linked polypropylene foam, is commonly categorized into two types based on density levels: low-density XPP foam and high-density XPP foam. Low-density XPP foam is lightweight and flexible, making it suitable for cushioning and packaging applications. On the other hand, high-density XPP foam is more rigid and provides enhanced durability and strength, making it ideal for structural applications such as automotive components. Both types of XPP foam offer unique properties that cater to a wide range of industries and applications.</p></p>
