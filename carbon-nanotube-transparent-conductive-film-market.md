<p><ul><li>Single-Walled Nanotubes (SWNTs)</li><li>Double Wall Nanotubes</li><li>Multi-Walled Nanotubes (MWNTs)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carbon Nanotube Transparent Conductive Film market consists of different types based on the structure of the nanotubes used. Single-Walled Nanotubes (SWNTs) are one-atom thick cylinders with high electrical conductivity. Double-Walled Nanotubes consist of two layers of carbon atoms, providing improved mechanical properties. Multi-Walled Nanotubes (MWNTs) have multiple layers of carbon atoms, offering enhanced conductivity and strength. Each type of nanotube has unique properties that cater to different applications in areas such as electronics, optoelectronics, and energy storage.</p></p>
