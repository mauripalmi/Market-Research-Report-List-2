<p><p>The Thermal Management System for Automotive Battery market includes three main types: Liquid Cooling, Air Cooling, and Refrigerant Cooling. Liquid Cooling involves circulating a liquid coolant through the battery system to regulate temperature. Air Cooling uses a fan or ventilation system to blow air over the battery cells to dissipate heat. Refrigerant Cooling utilizes a refrigerant fluid to absorb and remove heat from the battery. Each type of cooling system plays a crucial role in maintaining the optimal temperature of automotive batteries to ensure their performance and longevity.</p></p>
