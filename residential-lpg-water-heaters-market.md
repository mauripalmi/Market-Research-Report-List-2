<p><ul><li>Tankless LPG Water Heaters</li><li>Tank LPG Water Heaters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Residential LPG water heaters come in two main types: tankless LPG water heaters and tank LPG water heaters. Tankless LPG water heaters heat water on demand, providing hot water without the need for a storage tank. They are typically more energy efficient and take up less space than tank models. On the other hand, tank LPG water heaters store hot water in a tank, which allows for a consistent supply of hot water but may be less energy efficient due to standby heat loss.</p></p>
