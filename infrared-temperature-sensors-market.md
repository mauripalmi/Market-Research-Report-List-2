<p><p>Infrared temperature sensors consist of two main market types: contact type and non-contact type. Contact type sensors require physical contact with the object being measured to obtain accurate temperature readings. On the other hand, non-contact type sensors can measure temperatures from a distance without making direct contact with the object. These sensors are widely used in various industries such as manufacturing, automotive, healthcare, and food processing for monitoring and controlling temperature in real-time applications.</p></p>
