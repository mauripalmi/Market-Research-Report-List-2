<p><p>Polycarbonate glazing is available in two main market types: General Grade and High Transparency. General Grade polycarbonate glazing is commonly used for applications where impact resistance and durability are key factors, such as in construction and automotive industries. On the other hand, High Transparency polycarbonate glazing offers superior optical clarity and light transmission, making it ideal for applications where visibility and aesthetics are important, such as in greenhouses and architectural glazing. Both types of polycarbonate glazing provide excellent weather resistance and UV protection.</p></p>
