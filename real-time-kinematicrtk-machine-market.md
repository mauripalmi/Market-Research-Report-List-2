<p><p>Real Time Kinematic (RTK) Machine market can be classified into two types, namely Mobile RTK and Fixed RTK. Mobile RTK systems are mounted on vehicles such as drones or tractors, allowing for real-time positioning and navigation while moving. Fixed RTK systems are installed on a stationary base station and provide highly accurate positioning data to mobile users within a specific range. Both types of RTK systems are widely used in various industries such as agriculture, construction, and surveying for precise and efficient geospatial data collection and navigation.</p></p>
