<p><p>Foot orthosis (FO) market includes two main types: dynamic orthotics and static orthotics. Dynamic orthotics are designed to provide continuous and adjustable support, making them suitable for athletes and individuals with active lifestyles. On the other hand, static orthotics offer firm and fixed support, commonly used for conditions like plantar fasciitis and flat feet. Both types aim to improve foot function and reduce pain by correcting biomechanical issues, but they differ in their level of adjustability and flexibility.</p></p>
