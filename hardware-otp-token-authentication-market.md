<p><p>Hardware OTP tokens are authentication devices that generate one-time passwords for secure access to systems. The market for these tokens can be categorized into three main types: USB tokens, SIM tokens, and Mini tokens. USB tokens are portable devices that can be plugged into a computer's USB port for authentication. SIM tokens are integrated into a user's SIM card for authentication on mobile devices. Mini tokens are small, keychain-sized devices that provide secure access to systems. Each type offers unique benefits for different use cases in the market.</p></p>
