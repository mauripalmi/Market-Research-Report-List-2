<p><strong>The Distributed Acoustic Sensing Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DASI Type</li><li>DASP Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distributed Acoustic Sensing Systems (DAS) are divided into two main types: Distributed Acoustic Sensing Interrogator (DASI) and Distributed Acoustic Sensing Processor (DASP) systems. DASI systems consist of a fiber optic cable that acts as a sensor, detecting acoustic vibrations along its length. DASP systems process the acoustic data collected by DASI systems, providing real-time monitoring and analysis of various applications such as oil and gas pipelines, border security, and infrastructure monitoring. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing safety and security through advanced acoustic sensing technology.</p></p>
