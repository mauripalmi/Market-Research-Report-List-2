<p><p>Van aluminum alloy wheel market can be categorized into three main types: Casting, Forging, and Other. Casting wheels are manufactured using a casting process where molten aluminum is poured into a mold to shape the wheel. Forged wheels, on the other hand, are created by shaping solid pieces of aluminum through high-pressure forging techniques. The Other market segment includes wheels made using different manufacturing processes such as flow forming or rotary forging. Each type has its own advantages and characteristics, appealing to different consumer preferences.</p></p>
