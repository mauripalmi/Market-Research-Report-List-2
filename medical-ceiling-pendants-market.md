<p><p>Medical ceiling pendants are categorized into different types based on their mobility and flexibility. Fixed pendants are stationary and cannot be moved, while fixed retractable pendants can be extended or retracted as needed. Single arm movable pendants can move in a single direction, providing some flexibility in positioning. Double multi arm movable pendants offer the most mobility, with multiple arms that can be adjusted in various directions to accommodate different medical procedures. Each type of pendant offers specific advantages based on the healthcare facility's needs.</p></p>
