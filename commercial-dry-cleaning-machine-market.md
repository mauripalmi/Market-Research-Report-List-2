Single Solvent Dry Cleaning Machines use only one cleaning solvent, usually perchloroethylene, for cleaning garments. On the other hand, Multi Solvent Dry Cleaning Machines have the capability to use multiple solvents, providing more flexibility and options for different types of fabrics and cleaning needs. Both types cater to the diverse requirements of commercial dry cleaners in effectively cleaning garments.</p></p>
