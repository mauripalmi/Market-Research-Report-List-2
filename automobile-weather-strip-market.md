<p><ul><li>EPDM Strip</li><li>TPE/TPO/TPV Strip</li><li>Other Strip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile weather strip market includes three main types of strips: EPDM strip, TPE/TPO/TPV strip, and other strips. EPDM strips are made of a durable synthetic rubber known for its weather resistance. TPE/TPO/TPV strips are made of thermoplastic materials, offering flexibility and chemical resistance. Other strips can include silicone, neoprene, and PVC materials, each with their own unique properties. These different types cater to various automotive applications, providing sealing and insulation against weather elements for vehicle components.</p></p>
