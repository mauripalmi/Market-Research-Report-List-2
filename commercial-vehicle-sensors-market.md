<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Sensors Market includes various types of sensors such as MEMs (Microelectromechanical Systems), Pressure sensors, Temperature sensors, Position sensors, Motion sensors, Image sensors, Level sensors, and other sensors. MEMs sensors are used for measuring acceleration, pressure, and angular velocity. Pressure sensors monitor fluid pressure in the vehicle's systems. Temperature sensors measure the temperature of the engine or other components. Position sensors detect the position of various parts in the vehicle. Motion sensors track the movement of the vehicle. Image sensors capture visual information for driver assistance systems. Level sensors monitor the fluid levels in the vehicle. Other sensors include sensors for detecting proximity, light, and gas.</p></p>
