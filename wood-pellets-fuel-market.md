<p><p>Wood pellets fuel market can be categorized into two main types: White Pellet Market and Black Pellet Market. White pellets are made from sawdust and other clean wood waste, resulting in a high-quality and efficient fuel source. On the other hand, black pellets are produced from torrefied biomass, which undergoes a special heating process to increase its energy density. This makes black pellets a more sustainable and cost-effective option compared to traditional white pellets. Both markets cater to the growing demand for renewable and eco-friendly fuel sources.</p></p>
