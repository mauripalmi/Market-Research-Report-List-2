<p><p>The biogas dehumidifier market can be categorized into absorption dehumidification, condensation dehumidification, and others. Absorption dehumidification involves the use of a desiccant material to absorb moisture from the biogas stream. Condensation dehumidification utilizes a cooling system to condense water vapor in the biogas, extracting moisture from the gas stream. The "others" category includes other innovative technologies and processes for removing moisture from biogas, such as membrane filtration or adsorption dehumidification.</p></p>
