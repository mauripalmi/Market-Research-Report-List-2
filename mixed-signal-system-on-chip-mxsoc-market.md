<p><strong>The Mixed Signal System-on-Chip (MxSoC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard cell-based MxSoCs</li><li>Embedded design-based MxSoCs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Standard cell-based MxSoCs are integrated circuits that are designed using pre-designed, commercially available standard cells. These MxSoCs offer a cost-effective solution for mass production and are suitable for applications with high volume requirements. On the other hand, embedded design-based MxSoCs are customized chips that are designed specifically for a unique application. These MxSoCs offer higher performance and efficiency compared to standard cell-based MxSoCs but are more expensive and take longer to develop.</p></p>
