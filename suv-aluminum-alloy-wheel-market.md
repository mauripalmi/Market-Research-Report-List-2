<p><p>SUV aluminum alloy wheels are available in three main types: casting, forging, and other markets. Casting wheels are made by pouring molten aluminum into a mold, resulting in a more affordable option with a wide range of designs. Forged wheels are created by pressing aluminum billets under high pressure, offering superior strength and durability. The "other" market includes unique manufacturing processes such as flow-forming and rotary-forging, providing a mix of performance and affordability for consumers. Each type offers different benefits and features to cater to varying consumer needs in the SUV market.</p></p>
