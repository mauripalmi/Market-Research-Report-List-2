<p><p>Lithium-ion (Li-ion) Battery Materials market is divided into two main types; Cathode Materials and Anode Materials. Cathode materials are responsible for storing and releasing lithium ions during the battery's charge and discharge cycles. This includes materials such as lithium cobalt oxide, lithium iron phosphate, and lithium manganese oxide. On the other hand, Anode materials play a crucial role in the movement of lithium ions from the cathode to the anode. Common anode materials include graphite, silicon, and lithium titanate. Both cathode and anode materials are essential for the overall performance and efficiency of Li-ion batteries.</p></p>
