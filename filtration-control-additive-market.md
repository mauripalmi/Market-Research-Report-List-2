<p><strong>The Filtration Control Additive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymer Type</li><li>Non-polymer Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Filtration control additives can be categorized into two types: polymer type and non-polymer type. Polymer type additives are made of synthetic or natural polymers like polyacrylamides, guar gum, and xanthan gum, which are commonly used to control fluid loss and rheology in drilling fluids. On the other hand, non-polymer type additives include bentonite, silica flour, and diatomaceous earth, which are natural minerals used for controlling filtration and improving fluid properties. Both types of additives play a crucial role in enhancing filtration control in various industrial processes.</p></p>
