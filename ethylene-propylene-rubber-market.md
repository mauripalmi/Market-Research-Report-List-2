It is known for its excellent heat resistance and electrical insulating properties, making it ideal for applications in the automotive and construction industries. Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM) is another type of synthetic rubber derived from the polymerization of ethylene, propylene, and a small amount of diene monomers. EPDM offers superior resistance to weathering, ozone, and UV radiation, making it popular for roofing, automotive, and electrical applications.</p></p>
