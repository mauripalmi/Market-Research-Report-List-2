<p><p>Assembly adhesives are commonly used in various industries for bonding purposes. Two types of assembly adhesives widely used are paste and tape adhesives. Paste adhesives are typically applied using a dispenser or spread manually onto the surfaces to be bonded. They are commonly used for bonding irregular surfaces and materials. On the other hand, tape adhesives come in the form of pre-cut strips or rolls and are easy to apply by simply peeling off the backing and sticking onto the surfaces. They are ideal for bonding lightweight materials and for quick assembly applications.</p></p>
