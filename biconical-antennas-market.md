<p><p>Biconical antennas can be classified into two main types based on their coverage pattern: Omni-Directional Antenna and Directional Antenna. Omni-Directional antennas radiate and receive signals in all directions, making them suitable for applications where signals need to be transmitted in a 360-degree radius. On the other hand, Directional antennas focus their signal transmission and reception in a specific direction, providing higher gain and longer range. Both types of antennas cater to different needs and applications in the market.</p></p>
