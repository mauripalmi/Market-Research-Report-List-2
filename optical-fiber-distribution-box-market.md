<p><p>Optical Fiber Distribution Box Market Types include different optical splitters such as 1:4, 1:8, 1:16, and 1:32. These splitters are used to divide an incoming optical signal into multiple outputs, allowing for the distribution of the signal to various locations. The 1:4 splitter divides the signal into 4 outputs, while the 1:8 splitter divides it into 8 outputs, the 1:16 splitter divides it into 16 outputs, and the 1:32 splitter divides it into 32 outputs. These different splitter options cater to varying network requirements in terms of signal distribution.</p></p>
