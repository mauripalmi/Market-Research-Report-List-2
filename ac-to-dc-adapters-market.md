<p><p>AC-to-DC adapters come in two main market types based on their connector polarity: Center Positive (+) Connect Type and Center Negative (-) Connect Type. The Center Positive type has the center pin of the connector delivering a positive voltage, while the Center Negative type has the center pin delivering a negative voltage. These market types cater to different devices and applications that require specific polarity configurations for powering, ensuring compatibility and optimal performance. Selecting the correct type is crucial to avoid damaging devices or causing malfunctions.</p></p>
