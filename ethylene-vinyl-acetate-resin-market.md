<p><p>Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA) resin is used in various industries due to its flexibility, durability, and toughness. There are two main types of EVA resin markets - Tubular EVA and Autoclave EVA. Tubular EVA is produced using a high-pressure tubular reactor, resulting in a resin with uniform properties and high clarity. On the other hand, Autoclave EVA is made in autoclave reactors, which allows for more customization of properties such as density and melt index. Both types have their unique advantages and applications in different industries.</p></p>
