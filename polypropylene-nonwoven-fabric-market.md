<p><p>Polypropylene Nonwoven Fabric market consists of different types such as Spunbonded Fabric, Staples Fabric, Melt Blown Fabric, and Composite Fabric. Spunbonded Fabric is made by bonding polypropylene fibers together, Staples Fabric is produced by entangling fibers using mechanical, chemical, or thermal processes, Melt Blown Fabric is created by melting and extruding polypropylene fibers, and Composite Fabric is a combination of different fabrics for unique properties. Each type serves specific purposes in industries like medical, automotive, and construction due to their unique characteristics.</p></p>
