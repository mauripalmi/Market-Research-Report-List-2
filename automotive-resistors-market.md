<p><strong>The Automotive Resistors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Resistors</li><li>Voltage Limiting Resistors</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive resistors are categorized into various types based on their functions. Shunt resistors are used to measure electric current by creating a small voltage drop. Voltage limiting resistors are designed to limit the flow of current and protect sensitive components from damage in case of voltage surges. Other types of resistors in the automotive market include temperature sensors, motion sensors, and pressure sensors. These resistors play a crucial role in maintaining the proper functioning of various systems within a vehicle, ensuring safety and efficiency.</p></p>
