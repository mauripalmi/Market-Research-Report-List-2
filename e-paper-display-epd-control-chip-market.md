<p><strong>The E-paper Display (EPD) Control Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Discrete Controller</li><li>System on Chip (SoC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>E-paper Display (EPD) Control Chip market consists of two main types - Discrete Controller and System on Chip (SoC). Discrete Controller chips are standalone chips dedicated solely to controlling EPDs, providing a high level of customization and flexibility in design. On the other hand, System on Chip (SoC) integrates EPD control functions along with other related functions on a single chip, offering a more compact and cost-effective solution. Both types cater to different market needs and preferences in the EPD industry.</p></p>
