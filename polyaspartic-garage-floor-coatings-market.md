<p><p>Polyaspartic garage floor coatings can be categorized into two main types - Pure Coatings and Hybrid Coatings. Pure coatings are 100% polyaspartic formulations that provide superior chemical resistance and durability, making them ideal for high traffic areas. Hybrid coatings, on the other hand, combine polyaspartic resins with other components like polyurethane or epoxy to enhance specific properties like UV stability or adhesion. Both types offer excellent protection against wear and tear, providing long-lasting beauty for garage floors.</p></p>
