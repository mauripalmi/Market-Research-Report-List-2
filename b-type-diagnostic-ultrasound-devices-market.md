<p><p>B Type Diagnostic Ultrasound Devices market consists of three main types: Fanshaped Scanning, Linear Scanning, and Mixed Type. Fanshaped Scanning devices emit ultrasound waves in a fan-shaped pattern to capture images of organs like the heart. Linear Scanning devices use a linear probe to produce high-resolution images of structures near the skin surface. Mixed Type devices combine both fan-shaped and linear scanning capabilities for versatile imaging. Each type offers unique benefits for specific diagnostic applications within the healthcare industry.</p></p>
