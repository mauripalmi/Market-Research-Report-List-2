<p><strong>The Continuus Level Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Differential Pressure</li><li>Guided Wave Radar</li><li>Non-contact Radar</li><li>Ultrasonic</li><li>Capacitance</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous level monitors are available in various types to suit different industry needs. The market is dominated by differential pressure level monitors, which measure the pressure difference between the top and bottom of a vessel. Guided wave radar level monitors use radar waves to measure the level of the material. Non-contact radar level monitors use radar waves without touching the material. Ultrasonic level monitors use sound waves to measure the level. Capacitance level monitors measure the change in capacitance as the level changes. Other types include laser, microwave, and resistance-based level monitors.</p></p>
