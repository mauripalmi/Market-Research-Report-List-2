<p><p>Car video recorders come in two main types: single channel and multi-channel. Single channel recorders have only one camera that records the view from the front of the vehicle. Multi-channel recorders have multiple cameras that can capture footage from various angles around the car. Single channel recorders are typically more affordable and easier to install, while multi-channel recorders provide more comprehensive coverage and protection. Consumers can choose the type that best suits their needs and budget.</p></p>
