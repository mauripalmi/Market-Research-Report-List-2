<p><p>The Handheld Breath Alcohol Analyser market consists of two main types: Direct Sample and Passive Sample. Direct Sample analysers require the individual to breathe into the device for measurement, providing instantaneous results. Passive Sample analysers, on the other hand, detect alcohol levels in the surrounding air without requiring the person to blow into the device. This type is commonly used in law enforcement settings for non-invasive testing. Both types serve the purpose of quickly and accurately measuring alcohol levels for safety and legal compliance.</p></p>
