<p><strong>The Operating Room Sterilization Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Temperature Sterilization</li><li>Low Temperature Sterilization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Operating room sterilization equipment includes high temperature sterilization and low temperature sterilization methods. High temperature sterilization uses heat to eliminate microorganisms and is commonly achieved through autoclaves. On the other hand, low temperature sterilization methods such as ethylene oxide gas or hydrogen peroxide plasma are used for more heat-sensitive equipment. Both types of sterilization are crucial in maintaining a sterile surgical environment and preventing infections in operating rooms.</p></p>
