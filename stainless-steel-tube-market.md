<p><ul><li>Seamless Tube</li><li>Welded Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stainless steel tube market is divided into two main types: seamless tube and welded tube. Seamless tube is produced without a seam, providing a smoother surface finish and higher corrosion resistance. It is commonly used in high-pressure applications such as in the oil and gas industry. On the other hand, welded tube is created by welding two edges of a strip of stainless steel. It is more cost-effective and suitable for less demanding applications like construction and automotive industries.</p></p>
