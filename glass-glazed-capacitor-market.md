<p><ul><li>Silicon-lead Series</li><li>Silicon-titanium Series</li><li>Silicon-lead-titanium Series</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glass glazed capacitors come in three main types: Silicon-lead Series, Silicon-titanium Series, and Silicon-lead-titanium Series. The Silicon-lead Series capacitors are known for their low leakage current and stable capacitance, making them ideal for high-frequency applications. Silicon-titanium Series capacitors offer high reliability and low dielectric absorption, making them suitable for precision circuits. Silicon-lead-titanium Series capacitors combine the best characteristics of both types, providing excellent performance in various applications. Each type caters to specific needs in the market, offering different advantages for different uses.</p></p>
