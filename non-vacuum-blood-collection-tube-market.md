<p><p>Non vacuum blood collection tubes are categorized into different types based on their specific uses. Serum separating tubes are designed to separate serum from blood cells, while EDTA tubes contain a common anticoagulant for various blood tests. Plasma separation tubes are used to isolate plasma from blood cells. Other types of tubes include heparin tubes, citrate tubes, and glucose tubes, each serving a distinct purpose in blood collection and analysis. These different types cater to a wide range of medical testing requirements.</p></p>
