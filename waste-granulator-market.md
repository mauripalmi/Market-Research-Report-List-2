<p><ul><li>Beside-the-press Granulators</li><li>Central Granulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Beside-the-press Granulators are smaller, compact machines designed to be placed next to an injection molding machine or extruder to grind small sprues and runners. They are ideal for immediate recycling of small plastic waste. Central Granulators, on the other hand, are larger, more heavy-duty machines used for grinding larger plastic parts or bulky scraps in a centralized location within a facility. They are capable of processing a higher volume of waste material and are often used in larger industrial settings.</p></p>
