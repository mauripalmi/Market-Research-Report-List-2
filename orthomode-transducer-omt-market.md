<p><p>Orthomode transducer OMTs are used in microwave systems to separate incoming signals into their respective polarizations. There are two main market types based on the shape of the port: Square port OMTs and Circular port OMTs. Square port OMTs are popular for their ease of integration into rectangular waveguides, while Circular port OMTs are commonly used in circular waveguides for their ability to accommodate rotating mechanisms. Both market types cater to different system requirements and are widely used in various applications within the microwave industry.</p></p>
