<p><strong>The PPLN Waveguide Chips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Proton in-diffused (RPE) Waveguides</li><li>Ridge-Waveguides</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PPLN waveguide chips come in different market types such as Proton in-diffused (RPE) waveguides and Ridge-Waveguides. Proton in-diffused waveguides utilize a process where protons are diffused into the crystal structure to create waveguides, offering high efficiency and compact size. On the other hand, Ridge-Waveguides feature a ridge structure on the waveguide surface, providing better confinement of light and improved performance. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market for PPLN waveguide chips.</p></p>
