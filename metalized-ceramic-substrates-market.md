<p><ul><li>Molybdenum and Manganese Method</li><li>Gold Plating Method</li><li>Copper Plating Method</li><li>Tin Plating Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metalized ceramic substrates are used in various industries for their superior thermal and mechanical properties. The Molybdenum and Manganese Method involves a sputtering process to deposit a thin layer of metal on the ceramic surface. The Gold Plating Method uses gold as a coating material for enhanced conductivity. The Copper Plating Method utilizes copper for its excellent electrical properties. The Tin Plating Method involves depositing a layer of tin onto the ceramic substrate for improved corrosion resistance. Each method offers unique benefits for different applications in the market.</p></p>
