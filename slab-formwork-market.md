<p><p>Slab formwork market can be classified into different types based on the material used for construction. Timber formwork involves using timber beams and boards for supporting the concrete slab during construction. Steel formwork uses steel panels and props for the same purpose, offering high strength and durability. Aluminum formwork utilizes aluminum panels and beams for lightweight and easy-to-handle formwork solutions. Other markets in this sector can include plastic formwork, modular formwork systems, and fiberglass formwork, each catering to specific construction needs.</p></p>
