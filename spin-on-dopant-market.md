<p><p>Spin-on dopants are chemicals used in the semiconductor industry to modify the conductivity of silicon by introducing impurities. There are two main types of spin-on dopants: P type and N type. P type dopants introduce positively charged impurities into silicon, increasing its conductivity, while N type dopants introduce negatively charged impurities, also enhancing conductivity. Each type is used in specific applications in the manufacturing of semiconductors, depending on the desired electrical properties of the material being processed.</p></p>
