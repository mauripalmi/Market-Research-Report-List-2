<p><p>Power steering filters are essential components that help to keep the power steering system clean and well-maintained. There are two main types of power steering filters, direct fit filters, and in-line filters. Direct fit filters are designed to fit a specific make and model of vehicles, ensuring a perfect fit and optimum performance. In-line filters are universal filters that can be installed in various vehicles, providing flexibility and ease of use. Both types of filters serve the same purpose of protecting the power steering system from contaminants and prolonging its lifespan.</p></p>
