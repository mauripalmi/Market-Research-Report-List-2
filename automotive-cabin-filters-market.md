<p><p>The Automotive Cabin Filters Market includes three main types: Particle filters, Activated Carbon filters, and Electrostatic filters. Particle filters are designed to capture solid contaminants like dust and pollen. Activated Carbon filters use carbon to adsorb odors and gases. Electrostatic filters use static electricity to attract and trap particles. Each type offers different levels of air filtration and can improve air quality inside the vehicle. Consumers can choose the type of cabin filter based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
