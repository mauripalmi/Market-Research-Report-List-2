<p><ul><li>Single Type</li><li>Pair Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AM Electronic Article Surveillance Antennas market is divided into two main types: Single Type and Pair Type. Single Type antennas are used individually to detect tagged items passing through them. Pair Type antennas work in tandem, with one antenna transmitting a signal while the other receives it, creating a detection zone between them. Single Type antennas are more common in smaller retail stores, while Pair Type antennas are usually found in larger stores or shopping centers for wider coverage and more accurate detection of tagged items.</p></p>
