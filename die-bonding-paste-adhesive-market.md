<p><p>Die bonding paste adhesive market can be categorized into conductive and non-conductive types based on their electrical properties. Conductive paste adhesives are formulated with materials that allow for the flow of electricity, making them suitable for applications where electrical conductivity is necessary. On the other hand, non-conductive paste adhesives do not conduct electricity, making them ideal for applications where electrical insulation is required. Both types play a crucial role in the semiconductor industry, providing a reliable solution for bonding and attaching electronic components onto substrates.</p></p>
