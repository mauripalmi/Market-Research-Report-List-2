<p><p>Extracorporeal lithotripters are medical devices used for breaking down kidney stones with non-invasive shock waves. There are three main types of lithotripters: with lithotripsy table, with C-arm, and with endoscopy column. Lithotripters with a lithotripsy table are stationary machines with a treatment table, while those with a C-arm have imaging capabilities for guided stone targeting. Lithotripters with an endoscopy column are integrated with endoscopic equipment for simultaneous stone removal.</p></p>
