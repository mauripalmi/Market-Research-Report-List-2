<p><p>Fixed Cone Valves are flow control devices used in hydraulic structures such as dams, reservoirs, power plants, and irrigation systems. They consist of a fixed cone-shaped element attached to a movable gate that controls the flow of water by varying the gap between the cone and the gate. Fixed Cone Valves are known for their reliability, low maintenance requirements, and ability to handle high flow rates with minimal pressure loss.</p><p>The Fixed Cone Valves Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.2% during the forecast period.
