<p><ul><li>VZ Type</li><li>VU Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vinyl sheet piling market consists of two main types: VZ Type and VU Type. VZ Type sheet piling is designed with interlocking edges that provide a secure connection and stability when driven into the ground. It is commonly used in applications where high strength and durability are required. On the other hand, VU Type sheet piling features a smooth surface that allows for easy installation and removal. This type is often preferred for temporary or light-duty applications where quick installation is needed.</p></p>
