<p><p>The food industry extruder market comprises of two main types - single screw and twin screw extruders. Single screw extruders are simpler in design and less expensive, making them suitable for a wide range of food products. On the other hand, twin screw extruders offer enhanced mixing capabilities and can handle a wider variety of ingredients, making them ideal for more complex food products. Both types of extruders play a crucial role in food processing, with each offering unique advantages based on specific manufacturing needs.</p></p>
