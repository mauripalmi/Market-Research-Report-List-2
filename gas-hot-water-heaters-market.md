<p><ul><li>Tank Storage</li><li>Tankless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas hot water heaters come in two main types: tank storage and tankless. Tank storage heaters have a large reservoir of hot water that is constantly heated, providing a steady supply of hot water but taking up more space. On the other hand, tankless heaters heat water on demand, reducing energy consumption and saving space but may not provide as much hot water at once. Both types cater to different needs and preferences, offering options for consumers based on their priorities.</p></p>
