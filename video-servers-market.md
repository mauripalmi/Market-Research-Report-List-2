<p><p>Video servers market is segmented into M-JPEG, MPEG-4, and H.264 technologies. M-JPEG offers high-quality video with low latency, making it ideal for surveillance applications. MPEG-4 provides efficient compression for streaming video over networks, suitable for video conferencing and multimedia delivery. H.264 is the most advanced technology, offering high-quality video with the lowest bandwidth consumption, making it ideal for streaming high-definition content over the internet. Each technology caters to different needs in the video server market.</p></p>
