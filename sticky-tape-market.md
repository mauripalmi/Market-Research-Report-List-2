<p><p>Paper sticky tape is a type of adhesive tape that is commonly used for packaging and general sticking applications. It is made of a thin paper backing coated with adhesive on one side. Paper sticky tape is versatile, easy to use, and is available in various widths and lengths.</p><p>Fibre-reinforced tape, on the other hand, is a type of adhesive tape that is reinforced with fiberglass or other types of fibers. This makes the tape stronger and more durable, making it ideal for heavy-duty packaging and sealing applications. Fibre-reinforced tape is tear-resistant and offers superior strength compared to traditional paper sticky tape.</p></p>
