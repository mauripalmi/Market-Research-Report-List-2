<p><p>Agricultural milking robots can be categorized into three main types: Pail Milking Robots, Pipeline Milking Robots, and Other market. Pail Milking Robots are designed for small-scale operations, where each robot is responsible for milking one cow at a time. Pipeline Milking Robots are larger systems that can accommodate multiple cows at once, with a network of pipes delivering milk to a central collection point. The "Other" market includes various types of milking robots tailored for specific farm sizes or operational needs.</p></p>
