<p><strong>The Continuously Variable Transmission Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Transmission</li><li>Electric Transmission</li><li>Mechanical Transmission</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) devices come in three main types: Liquid Transmission, Electric Transmission, and Mechanical Transmission. Liquid Transmission CVTs use hydraulic fluid to transfer power between the engine and wheels. Electric Transmission CVTs utilize electric motors to smoothly vary the gear ratio. Mechanical Transmission CVTs, the most common type, use a system of belts or chains to achieve infinite gear ratios. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in various industries, providing efficient and seamless power transfer.</p></p>
