<p><ul><li>Cold Water-soluble Films</li><li>Hot Water-soluble Films</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVA water-soluble films are categorized into two main types: cold water-soluble films and hot water-soluble films. Cold water-soluble films dissolve in water at lower temperatures, making them suitable for use in products like detergents and packaging. On the other hand, hot water-soluble films dissolve at higher temperatures, often used in products like embroidery backing and agrochemical packaging. Both types offer the convenience of easy dissolution in water, making them environmentally friendly alternatives for various applications.</p></p>
