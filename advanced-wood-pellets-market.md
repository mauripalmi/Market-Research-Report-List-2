<p><ul><li>Torrefaction</li><li>Steam Explosion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Advanced wood pellets are expanding to include different market types such as torrefaction and steam explosion. Torrefaction involves heating the biomass at high temperatures in the absence of oxygen to create a more energy-dense product. Steam explosion, on the other hand, uses high-pressure steam to break down the biomass into smaller components for easier processing. These advanced wood pellet market types offer potential benefits such as increased energy efficiency, reduced transportation costs, and improved combustion properties.</p></p>
