<p><p>Disposable intravenous line connectors are medical devices used to connect IV tubing and other medical devices to the patient's venous system. There are three main types of connectors - Y shaped, T shaped, and V shaped. The Y shaped connector allows for two branches of tubing to be connected to one main line. The T shaped connector has three branches, with one main line and two secondary lines. The V shaped connector is designed with three branches in a V shape, allowing for multiple connections to be made.</p></p>
