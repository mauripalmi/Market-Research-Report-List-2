<p><p>Sintered NdFeB magnets are classified into different types based on their coercivity level, which determines their magnetic strength and performance. Low Coercivity N magnets are the weakest, followed by Medium Coercivity M, High Coercivity H, Ultra-high Coercivity SH, UH Ultra-high Coercivity, and the strongest are the High Coercivity magnets. Each type serves specific industries and applications, with higher coercivity magnets being preferred for demanding environments where stronger magnetic force is required.</p></p>
