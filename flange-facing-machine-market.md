<p><ul><li>OD Mounted Flange Facing Machines</li><li>ID Mounted Flange Facing Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flange Facing Machines are divided into two main types based on their mounting style: OD mounted and ID mounted. OD mounted machines are positioned on the outside of the flange and are used for facing the outer diameter of the flange. On the other hand, ID mounted machines are positioned inside the flange and are used for facing the inner diameter of the flange. Each type of machine caters to specific needs and requirements in the market for flange facing operations.</p></p>
