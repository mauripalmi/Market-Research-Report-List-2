<p><p>The personal radiation monitor market consists of two main types: Active and Passive. </p><p>Active monitors continuously measure radiation levels in real-time, providing instant feedback to the user. These monitors are typically worn by individuals working in high-risk environments where radiation exposure is a concern. </p><p>Passive monitors, on the other hand, collect radiation data over a certain period of time and require analysis after the monitoring period is complete. These monitors are often used for routine monitoring in less hazardous environments. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety of individuals exposed to radiation.</p></p>
