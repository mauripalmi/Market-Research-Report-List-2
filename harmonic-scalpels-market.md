<p><ul><li>Attracting Harmonic Scalpels</li><li>Cutting Harmonic Scalpels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Harmonic scalpels market is divided into two main types based on their functions - Attracting harmonic scalpels and Cutting harmonic scalpels. Attracting harmonic scalpels are designed to coagulate blood vessels during surgical procedures, reducing bleeding and improving visibility for the surgeon. Cutting harmonic scalpels, on the other hand, are used for precise and efficient cutting of tissues. Both types of harmonic scalpels are widely used in various surgical specialties for their benefits in reducing operative time and providing better patient outcomes.</p></p>
